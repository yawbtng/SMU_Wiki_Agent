# Student Wiki Content Policy

The student wiki should prioritize current, canonical, student-actionable information.

## Keep Or Promote

- Registrar, enrollment, academic calendars, and final exams.
- Course catalog, degree/program requirements, and courses.
- Grades, GPA, probation/suspension, withdrawal, and drop policies.
- Tuition, financial aid, scholarships, billing, and payment.
- Housing, dining, health, counseling, parking, orientation, and accessibility.

## Exclude Or Demote

- Class notes, alumni updates, and old dated news or magazine articles.
- Donor/giving/advancement pages, annual reports, president/trustee/admin pages.
- Staff bios unless they are clearly student-support contact pages.
- Design/component/template/demo/search pages.
- Corrupted extraction artifacts or pages that are mostly navigation/boilerplate.

## Stale Content Handling

For stale-content questions, recommend refresh discovery/scrape, re-normalize sources, purge excluded artifacts, and rebuild the wiki/index cleanly.
